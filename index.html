About the App

How does this work?

Search or scan a product and we will find you multiple retailers that sell that same product. We leave it up to you to figure out which retailer is best for your needs. Once you decide on a retailer, we send you to their app (or website) and let them handle it from there.

Found a irresistable deal? Share it with your friends and community. Making a checklist, wishlist, or shopping list? Check out our collaborative lists. What about browsing around? Check out our category pages. Maybe you want to know the community favoites? Check out our "Hottest Products" list, updated everyday.

Wishing we had a feature that is not listed? Talk to us! We are human and building this app specifically for you!

Features



Keyword Searching

Search your favorite products by name or keyword and we will find the best matches across multiple retailers.

Barcode Scan

Wanting to see if a product has a better price at another store? Scan the barcode and we will find retailers with the same product.

Lists & Favorites

Create lists to save your favorite products. Keep your list up to date by checking off products you no longer need.

Share

Share products to your feed so your friends don't miss out on the deals you find! Copy links to products directly to share with others via email, text, etc.

Community

See what the hottest products are in our community! Check the "Hottest Deals" page (updated daily) to see which deals are fan favorites.

Discover

Use our Categories page to browse across multiple categories, with hundreds of products. Find something interesting? Share it with the community!
